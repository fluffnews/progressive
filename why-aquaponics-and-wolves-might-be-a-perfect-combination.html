Why Aquaponics and Wolves Might Be a Perfect Combination

The Progressive Open News Network | Wed 04 January 2023

Aquaponics

Aquaponics is a method of growing plants in water without the use of soil. The plants are fed by nutrients that come from fish waste. Aquaponics is a sustainable way of growing food that doesn't require the use of pesticides or other harmful chemicals.

Wolves

Wolves are a keystone species that play an important role in the ecosystem. They help to control the population of deer and other prey animals. Wolves also help to keep the forest healthy by eating sick and weak animals.

Why Aquaponics and Wolves Might Be a Perfect Combination

Aquaponics and wolves might be a perfect combination because they are both sustainable and environmentally friendly. Aquaponics doesn't require the use of pesticides or other harmful chemicals, and wolves help to keep the forest healthy by eating sick and weak animals.
